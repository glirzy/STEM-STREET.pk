    Computing and programming is the future 
    give your kids an early start!

    Join the STEM Street Clubhouse, where kids aged 6-16 can learn coding with Scratch and Python, control electronic components, and discover basic machine learning concepts. Enable your child to develop problem-solving skills, creativity, and a love for STEM in a fun and engaging environment!

    Learn More

    Learn Scratch Programming

    Develop a strong foundation in coding through Scratch, enabling children to create interactive games, animations, and stories while enhancing their problem-solving and computational thinking skills.

    Understand Electronics & Digital-Making

    Gain hands-on experience in  controlling electronic components, understanding circuit design, microcontrollers, and sensors, which helps children make sense of the digital world around them.

    Grasp Basic Machine Learning Concepts

    Learn the fundamentals of machine learning, empowering children to create and integrate custom models using Teachable Machine and Scratch, and inspiring them to explore the potential of artificial intelligence.

    Develop 21st Century Skills:

    Cultivate essential skills such as communication, collaboration, critical thinking, and creativity through project-based learning, preparing children for success in a rapidly evolving STEM landscape.

    Why should my child learn computing and programming? 

    In today's fast-paced, digital world, learning coding skills from an early age is more important than ever. By nurturing these abilities, children can unlock their creativity, sharpen critical thinking, and gain a competitive edge for future academic and professional success. Empower your child to embrace the challenges of the digital era and become a confident innovator.
